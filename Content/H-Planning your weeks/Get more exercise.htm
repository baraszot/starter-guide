Get more exercise

You will probably be sitting at your computer more than before and so you’ll probably need to be paying more attention to how much you move. If you’re not a sporty person by nature, fear not: that doesn’t mean you need a crazy fitness regime. You’re not training for the Olympics. But you do need some muscle work to avoid back pain, help your metabolism (which, btw, is helping a life-sustaining mechanism, not just burning calories to lose weight as we're constantly being told in ads) and to learn better. Research suggests that little doses of movement done often are in fact more beneficial than big chunks of it done sporadically. So while some more targeted exercise is great for you, remember to seek out opportunities to move more in your everyday life.
