Mobile app

Other than the desktop app version, Teams can also be downloaded to your phone or tablet. You’re by no means required to download it, but it may come in handy in some cases. On some occasions, you may need to leave before the class finishes and using the app on your phone will allow you to stay connected. You can listen to the lecture, see the shared content and, if your circumstances allow it, join the conversation. For some of the more theoretical classes this should be enough not to miss out on anything.

The mobile app may also be more efficient in making you aware of anything new happening on Teams – it will notify you of any activity such as new posts or chat messages by your instructors or your fellow students.

Last, but not least, it can be used in all sorts of emergencies when something goes wrong with your computer – you can choose to be connected with both your desktop and your mobile app at the same time. This can help a lot when, for example, you suddenly lose sound in your desktop app.
