Additional devices

You will primarily need a PC to participate in the classes; ideally with Windows as some of the tools will require setting up a virtual machine if you’re a Mac user (no worries, though, if that’s your case – the instructors are used to there usually being some Mac users and will provide you with all the help you need). What may also come handy is (most of the time) a second screen and (sometimes) a mobile device with MS Teams installed.
