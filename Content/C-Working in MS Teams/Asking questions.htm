Asking questions

If you want to indicate that you have a question or a comment without interrupting the speaker, you can raise your virtual hand. You will find the appropriate button among the main meeting controls at the top bar. Once you click it, a little hand icon will appear on your video and next to your name on the list of the participants and a notification will be shown to the group. If you no longer wish to speak, you can lower your hand the same way you raised it.

While technically everyone in the meeting can see your raised hand, if the instructor is sharing their screen in the midst of explaining something, they may simply not see the incoming notification or the hand icon.
