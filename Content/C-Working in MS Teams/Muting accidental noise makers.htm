Muting accidental noise makers

As humans, we’ve probably made massive progress in the art of muting and unmuting ourselves over the last few years, and yet mishaps still occur. If someone left their mic on by accident and the noises coming from their room are disturbing you and the rest of the group, you can mute them yourself (if they’re busy negotiating with their toddler, they’re probably not paying attention to what’s happening on Teams). You can do that by clicking on the user menu (the three dots that will appear next to the participant’s name when you move your mouse over their video) and choosing Mute participant.
