Working in MS Teams

The live online classes are conducted via MS Teams. The invites will be sent to you on a rolling basis by respective instructors: some do it well in advance, some may wait till the evening before the class. When you log in around the time of the start of the meeting, Teams will most likely prompt you to join with a pop-up window.

For other ways to join, you can click the link in the invite message (found under Teams in the left-hand side vertical panel in the Teams app), click the link in the e-mail notification on the invite (if you have received it) or join in through the link in the Calendar (also found in the left-hand side vertical panel in the Teams app).

If you don’t see the meeting in your Calendar, even though the instructor has already posted the meeting details on Teams, you can add it manually:

  1. Open the meeting details window. You can do that by:
    1. double-clicking the bar with the meeting’s title;
    2. choosing View meeting details in the menu on the bar.

  2. Click Add to calendar in the meeting details window.
