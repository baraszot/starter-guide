Ensuring your background is presentable

You’ve only tidied half of your room and you’re not sure if it will show on camera? Fear not, Teams allow you to preview your video before others see it.

Before joining the meeting, in the Choose your video and audio options window, switch the camera on and make sure everything looks ok to you. You can also see the Background filters there, should you want to hide your actual background from other participants. Once you’re satisfied with the overall look, proceed to join the meeting with the Join now button on the right.

The preview is also available to you when you are already in the meeting but have your camera off. In the Camera options, choose Private preview and enjoy the benefit of double-checking whether you’re not showing something you don’t want to.

You do not have to apply a background filter simply because you think your home is not impressive enough. And don’t choose them because they’re fun. Your background should be first and foremost reasonably neutral to your fellow participants. If you expect your family or your housemates may be moving around behind you, consider a filter. If you don’t think you need it, you don’t have to use one just because other participants do. Don’t try to entertain others by choosing the craziest one. Even if they find it amusing at first, they may not appreciate the distraction during the fifth hour when their tired brain is prone to wandering off.
