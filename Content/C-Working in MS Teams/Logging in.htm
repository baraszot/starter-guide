Logging in

Keep in mind, that you should log in with your institutional account (i.e. use the e-mail you have at stu.vistula.edu.pl). Using a different account (which may be the account your Teams desktop app uses by default) will mean you log in as guest, with no access to chat and screen sharing. As a guest, you will also need someone to let you in the meeting (as opposed to being automatically allowed in with your institutional account).

However, if your app just won’t cooperate on a given day and you only manage to join in the guest mode, do not stress too much about it. Everything posted in the chat is saved in the app. You can access it later. Also even though you do need someone to let you in, it does not have to be the meeting organizer. Everyone will see the notification that you are trying to get in and everyone logged in with their institutional accounts have the authority to allow that. Even if the instructor fails to notice your request to join, your fellow students probably won’t keep you waiting for long.
