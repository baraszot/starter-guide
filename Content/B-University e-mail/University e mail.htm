University e-mail

At the beginning of your studies, the university will provide you with an institutional e-mail address linked to a free MS Office 365 Education account. When opening mail.vistula.edu.pl, you will be redirected to your Office account. The vertical panel on the left-hand side contains links to the most popular apps, including the ones you will be using most often: Outlook and Teams.

You should be using your institutional account to log in to Teams for your classes. Choosing online version of Teams from the left panel when accessing through mail.vistula.edu.pl should log you in correctly. When you log in from a desktop app, double check if you’re using the correct account.

Logging in with your private e-mail address is possible, but you will only be admitted to the meeting as a guest. This comes with some restrictions: you will need to be admitted in by somebody (rather than be taken straight to the meeting), you won’t be able to share your screen or see the meeting’s chat.
