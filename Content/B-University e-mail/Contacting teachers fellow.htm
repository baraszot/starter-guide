Contacting teachers, fellow students, and administrative staff

Every Vistula student and employee has their institutional e-mail address and you can easily find them when using the Outlook app. When writing a message, simply start typing the name of the person you want to write to in the To field and you will automatically get suggestions that match what you have already typed.

Please note that while everyone involved with your studies has a Vistula account, not everybody checks it on a regular basis. The instructors who do not, will most probably inform you about their preferred way to be contacted. If you yourself do not plan to log in to your institutional Outlook very often, forwarding your mail to an account that you regularly use might be a good idea (see Forwarding e-mails to your private e‑mail account).
