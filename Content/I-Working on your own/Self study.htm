Self-study

The instructors will be giving you tips on where to look for more information regarding technical writing in general as well as specific aspects of it that relate to material covered in class. You can expect a list of podcasts, blogs, articles and books that you can use to broaden your horizons. You can try to set up a habit of listening to a podcast while you’re doing something that allows you to listen to a podcast (on your commute to work, while cleaning, etc.) or aim to read an article or a blog entry every couple of days. Just try not to overdo it – remember you need some proper downtime to be able to process all the new information.
