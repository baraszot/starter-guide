Go offline

The only reason to stay at your computer during break is when you have a chat with someone from the meeting. If you’re not doing that, get up and move away from your computer (and your phone!) for a bit.

When we’re stressed, our vision tends to narrow so that we can concentrate on the perceived danger we are faced with. But as Andrew Huberman argues, the process may work both ways – if we narrow our vision by staring at a screen for a long time, you may end up feeling more stressed and anxious.
